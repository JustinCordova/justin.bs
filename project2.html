Tyler's Life

Tyler's Life is a 2D adventure RPG set in a vibrant, pixel-art world where you take on the role of Tyler, an ordinary person with extraordinary potential. Inspired by classic games like Pokémon, this early-stage project invites players to explore an expansive world filled with quirky characters, mysterious creatures, and challenging obstacles. As Tyler, you’ll embark on a journey of discovery, collecting items, solving puzzles, and building relationships with the diverse inhabitants of the world. Your choices will shape your adventure, with battles, exploration, and personal growth at its core. Whether you're wandering through lush forests, navigating bustling towns, or uncovering hidden secrets, Tyler's Life promises an engaging and nostalgic experience that blends the charm of retro games with a fresh, modern twist.
